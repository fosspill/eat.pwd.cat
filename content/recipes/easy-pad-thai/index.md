ingredients:
- 2 packages ramen noodles (oriental or chicken flavors work best)
- 350 grams frozen mixed vegetables
- 70 ml teriyaki sauce
- 2 tbsp hot water
- 3 tbsp creamy peanut butter
- 0.25 tsp sriracha or any hot sauce 

directions:
- In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil and cook ramen noodles according to package directions, reserving flavor packets. Drain noodles and set aside.
- Meanwhile, in a large skillet over medium-high heat, add oil, frozen vegetables and reserved flavor packets and cook until warm and cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and add noodles to skillet.
- Whisk together teriyaki sauce, hot water, peanut butter and sriracha; toss with noodles and vegetable mixture. Sprinkle each serving with red pepper flakes, if desired.

Ramen noodles + frozen mixed veggies + savory peanut sauce = Delicious.
